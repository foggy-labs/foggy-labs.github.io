Frequently Asked Questions

How do streaks work?
Each day you log a habit, your streak increases by one. Miss a day and your streak resets—unless you use a streak freeze. Long streaks build momentum and make it harder to break the chain.
What are streak freezes?
Streak freezes let you skip a day without losing your streak. Free users get a limited number of freezes. Pro users get more frequent freezes plus streak insurance for extended protection.
How do community features work?
You can join challenges, share your streak progress, and connect with friends building similar habits. Community features are available to all users, with expanded access for Pro subscribers.
Can I set habits to something other than daily?
Yes. Set habits to daily, weekly, or a custom schedule. Linkt adapts the streak logic to match your chosen frequency—so a habit set to 3x per week tracks accordingly.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to iPhone Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > Linkt. You can cancel anytime and keep Pro access until the end of your billing period.

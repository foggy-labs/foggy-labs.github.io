FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How do I add a supplement? +
Tap the add button on the main screen, enter the supplement name, choose the time of day (morning, afternoon, or evening), and save. It will appear in your daily checklist immediately.
How do streaks work? +
Your streak counts consecutive days where you checked off every scheduled supplement. Miss a day and the streak resets — but your longest streak is always saved so you can try to beat your record.
Can I track supplements by time of day? +
Yes. Every supplement is assigned to a time slot — morning, afternoon, or evening. Your daily checklist groups them by time so you always know what to take and when.
Is my data private? +
Completely. StackDay stores all data locally on your device using SwiftData. Nothing is uploaded to any server, and no account is required. Your supplement data never leaves your iPhone.
How do I cancel my subscription? +
Go to iPhone Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → StackDay. You can cancel anytime and keep access until the end of your billing period.
Does the app work without internet? +
Yes, completely. StackDay is 100% offline. All your data is stored on your device and the app never needs an internet connection to work. No network required, ever.

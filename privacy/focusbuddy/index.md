# FocusBuddy - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: April 4, 2026**

FocusBuddy ("we," "us," or "our") operates the FocusBuddy mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App. Please read this policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the App.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide Directly

**Focus Session Data.** You generate focus session data when you use the timer, including session start and end times, duration, room selection (Café, Library, Co-working), timer phase (work, break, long break), and completion status. All of this data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not upload your session data to our servers or to third-party servers.

**Communications.** If you contact our support team, we collect the content of your messages and any information you provide.

### Information Collected Automatically

**Purchase Data.** When you subscribe to FocusBuddy Pro, Apple processes your payment and provides us with a transaction receipt to verify your subscription status. We do not receive or store your payment method details.

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number (the App does not require an account).
- We do not upload your focus session history or timer data to our servers or to any third-party servers.
- We do not collect analytics, usage data, or behavioral tracking data.
- We do not collect precise geolocation data or track your real-time location.
- We do not access your contacts, camera, or photos.
- We do not use the microphone — the App generates ambient sounds procedurally using the device's audio engine.
- We do not use device fingerprinting or advertising identifiers (IDFA).
- We do not share personal data with third-party AI models or services.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide, operate, and maintain the App and its features, including focus session tracking, ambient soundscapes, and Pomodoro timer functionality
- Verify your subscription status to unlock premium features
- Communicate with you about support requests
- Comply with legal obligations

## 3. Local Data Storage

All focus session data in FocusBuddy is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. Your session history, streaks, and timer preferences never leave your device. We do not maintain any server-side database of your data.

## 4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

| Service | Purpose | Data Shared |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| Apple StoreKit | In-app purchases and subscriptions | Purchase transactions, subscription status |

We do not sell your personal data to any third party. We do not use advertising networks, analytics services, or attribution tracking.

## 5. Data Retention

- Focus session data is stored on-device and never stored on our servers.
- Communication records (support emails) are retained for up to 24 months.

When you delete the App, all locally stored data is removed from your device.

## 6. Data Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect your data, including:

- All session data is stored locally on your device — it never leaves your device
- All data stored locally using iOS system-level encryption
- No server-side storage of your data

No method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

## 7. Your Rights and Choices

- **Access.** Your data is stored on your device and accessible to you directly within the App at all times.
- **Deletion.** Deleting the App removes all locally stored data.

To exercise any rights or with questions, please contact us at ben@thefoghill.com. We will respond within 30 days.

## 8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that data promptly. If you believe that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us at ben@thefoghill.com.

## 9. International Data Transfers

FocusBuddy stores all data locally on your device. We do not transfer your data to any servers. In the event you contact us via email, your communication may be processed in the United States.

## 10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA):

- The right to know what personal data we collect, use, and disclose
- The right to request deletion of your personal data
- The right to opt out of the sale of personal data — we do not sell your personal data
- The right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at ben@thefoghill.com.

## 11. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, we process your data under the following legal bases:

- **Contract performance:** To provide the App and its features as described in our Terms of Service
- **Legitimate interests:** To ensure security and prevent fraud

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Rectify inaccurate personal data
- Request erasure of your personal data
- Restrict or object to the processing of your personal data
- Data portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
- Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent

You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority if you believe your rights have been violated.

To exercise these rights, contact us at ben@thefoghill.com. We will respond to verified requests within 30 days.

## 12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy in the App and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

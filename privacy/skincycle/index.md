# SkinCycle - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: April 1, 2026**

SkinCycle ("we," "us," or "our") operates the SkinCycle mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App. Please read this policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the App.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide Directly

**Skincare Data.** You provide information about your skincare products (names, categories, cycle frequencies) and routines (names, schedules, completion logs). This data is stored locally on your device and optionally synced via iCloud.

**Photos.** You may optionally attach photos of your skincare products. These are stored locally on your device and optionally synced via iCloud.

**Communications.** If you contact our support team, we collect the content of your messages and any information you provide.

### Information Collected Automatically

**Usage Data.** We collect information about how you interact with the App, including screens viewed, features used, actions taken, the dates and times of your visits, and session duration.

**Device Information.** We collect device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and general device settings such as language and timezone.

**Crash and Performance Data.** We collect crash logs, error reports, and performance metrics to diagnose and fix issues.

**Attribution Data.** We collect information about how you discovered and installed the App, including which advertisement or campaign led to your install.

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do not collect precise geolocation data or track your real-time location.
- We do not access your contacts, microphone, or health data.
- We do not use device fingerprinting or advertising identifiers (IDFA) for tracking purposes beyond install attribution.
- We do not share personal skincare data with third-party services.
- We do not share personal data with third-party AI models or services.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide, operate, and maintain the App and its features, including product tracking, routine management, and streak analytics
- Process your subscriptions and manage premium features
- Send you push notifications for skincare routine reminders (you can disable these at any time in your device settings)
- Monitor and analyze usage trends and preferences to improve the App
- Measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns and understand how users discover the App
- Detect, prevent, and address technical issues, fraud, and abuse
- Communicate with you about updates, support requests, and promotional offers related to the App (with your consent where required by law)
- Comply with legal obligations

## 3. iCloud Sync

Your skincare data (products, routines, and completion logs) may be synced to iCloud using Apple's CloudKit framework if you are signed in to iCloud on your device. This data is stored in your private iCloud container and is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. Foggy Labs does not have access to your iCloud data.

## 4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each of which has its own privacy policy governing the data it processes:

| Service | Purpose | Data Shared |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| PostHog | Product analytics | Anonymous usage data, screen views, feature interactions, session data |
| Sentry | Crash reporting and error monitoring | Device info, crash logs, anonymized usage context |
| AppsFlyer | Install attribution and marketing analytics | Device identifiers (where permitted), install source, in-app conversion events |
| Facebook SDK (Meta) | Advertising attribution and App Events | Install attribution data, anonymized conversion events |
| Apple CloudKit | iCloud data sync | Your skincare data (stored in your private iCloud container) |
| Apple StoreKit | In-app purchases and subscriptions | Purchase transactions, subscription status |

We do not sell your personal data to any third party. We do not share your personal skincare data with advertising networks. We do not provide your data to third-party AI models or machine learning services.

All third-party service providers are contractually required to protect your data with safeguards equal to or greater than those described in this Privacy Policy.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as the App is installed or as needed to provide you services. Specifically:

- Skincare product, routine, and log data is stored locally on your device and in your private iCloud container.
- Analytics data is retained for up to 12 months.
- Crash and performance logs are retained for up to 90 days.
- Communication records (support emails) are retained for up to 24 months.

When you delete the App, locally stored data is removed from your device. iCloud data can be deleted by removing the App from your iCloud account.

## 6. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal data, including:

- All data stored locally using SwiftData with iOS system-level encryption
- iCloud data encrypted by Apple in transit and at rest
- No server-side storage of your skincare data

No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

## 7. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

- **Access.** Your skincare data is visible to you directly within the App at all times.
- **Deletion.** You can delete individual products, routines, or logs within the App. Deleting the App removes all locally stored data.
- **Opt Out of Notifications.** You can disable push notifications at any time through your device's settings or within the App under Settings.
- **Opt Out of Analytics.** You can limit analytics collection by disabling "Allow Apps to Request to Track" in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.

To exercise any of these rights or with questions, please contact us at ben@thefoghill.com. We will respond within 30 days.

## 8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that data promptly. If you believe that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us at ben@thefoghill.com.

## 9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your data to these jurisdictions, which may have data protection laws that differ from those in your country.

## 10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA):

- The right to know what personal data we collect, use, and disclose
- The right to request deletion of your personal data
- The right to opt out of the sale of personal data — we do not sell your personal data
- The right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at ben@thefoghill.com.

## 11. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, we process your data under the following legal bases:

- **Contract performance:** To provide the App and its features as described in our Terms of Service
- **Legitimate interests:** To improve the App, prevent fraud, and ensure security
- **Consent:** For optional features like push notifications

You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority if you believe your rights have been violated.

## 12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy in the App and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

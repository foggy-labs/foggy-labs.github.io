The Problem

NYC has hundreds of galleries. You can't keep up.

You hear about an exhibition. You mean to go. Then you forget, or it closes, or you lose the name in a sea of Instagram stories and email newsletters. There's no great way to track what you've seen, what you want to see, or what's even on.

How it works

Your art practice in three steps.

1

Discover what's on

Browse the Explore tab for exhibitions happening now across Manhattan, Brooklyn, and Queens. Filter by neighborhood or art type.

2

Save it before it closes

Tap the bookmark on any exhibition to save it. Your list lives on your device — private, always accessible, no account required.

3

Log your visits

After you go, mark it as visited. Over time, Gallery becomes a living record of your relationship with NYC art.

🔒

Your data never leaves your device.

All your exhibition saves, gallery visit logs, and follows are stored locally using Apple's SwiftData framework. We don't maintain a database of your curation activity. No account required. No cloud sync. No ads. Your art life stays yours.

FAQ

Common questions.

Is Gallery free?

Yes, Gallery is completely free. No subscriptions, no in-app purchases. Just download and use.

Do I need an account?

No. All your data is stored locally on your device. No sign-up, no email, no password.

Where does the exhibition data come from?

Gallery sources exhibition data from curated NYC art databases. Always verify hours directly with the gallery before visiting.

What iPhone do I need?

Gallery requires iOS 17 or later. Works on all modern iPhones. iPad support coming soon.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How does the Pomodoro timer work?**
Start a focus session (25 minutes by default), then take a short break (5 minutes). After 4 completed sessions, take a long break (15 minutes). This cycle helps you maintain deep focus while preventing burnout.

**What are the ambient rooms?**
FocusBuddy offers 3 ambient soundscapes to help you concentrate: **Café** (coffee shop murmur), **Library** (quiet page turns), and **Co-working** (gentle keyboard clicks). The Café room is free; Library and Co-working require FocusBuddy Pro.

**How many free sessions do I get per day?**
Free users can run up to 3 focus sessions per day. Upgrade to FocusBuddy Pro for unlimited sessions.

**How does Focus Mode integration work?**
With FocusBuddy Pro, the app can activate your iPhone's Focus Mode during sessions to automatically suppress notifications. You'll be prompted to grant permission the first time. You can manage this in your iPhone Settings > Focus.

**Can I export my session history?**
Yes! FocusBuddy Pro includes CSV export of your focus session history. Go to Settings > Export Sessions to download your data.

**How do I manage my subscription?**
Go to your iPhone Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > FocusBuddy. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel from there.

**How do I restore my purchases?**
Open FocusBuddy > Settings > Restore Purchases. This will restore any active subscriptions associated with your Apple ID.

**What happens if I delete the app?**
All your focus session history and preferences are stored locally on your device. Deleting the app will permanently remove all data. We recommend exporting your session history first if you want to keep it.

**I found a bug. How do I report it?**
Email us at ben@thefoghill.com with a description of the issue, your device model, and iOS version. Screenshots help!

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I create a skincare routine?**
Tap the "+" button on the Routines screen. Choose AM or PM, add your products in the order you apply them, and save. SkinCycle will remind you to complete your routine each day.

**How does product cycling work?**
Some products (like retinoids or exfoliants) shouldn't be used every day. SkinCycle lets you set a cycle frequency for each product — for example, every other day or twice a week — and adjusts your daily routine accordingly.

**Can I track multiple routines?**
Yes! Create separate AM and PM routines, plus custom routines for specific days or occasions. Pro users can create unlimited routines.

**Does SkinCycle sync across devices?**
SkinCycle uses iCloud to sync your data across your Apple devices. Make sure you're signed in to iCloud on all devices where you want your data to appear.

**How do I manage my subscription?**
Go to your iPhone Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > SkinCycle. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel from there. You can also manage your subscription from the Settings tab within the app.

**How do I restore my purchases?**
Open SkinCycle > Settings > Restore Purchases. This will restore any active subscriptions associated with your Apple ID.

**What happens if I delete the app?**
Locally stored data is removed from your device. If you use iCloud sync, your data remains in your iCloud account and will restore when you reinstall the app.

**I found a bug. How do I report it?**
Email us at ben@thefoghill.com with a description of the issue, your device model, and iOS version. Screenshots help!

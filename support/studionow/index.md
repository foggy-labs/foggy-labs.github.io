## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I create an artist profile?**
Open StudioNow, tap "Get Started," and follow the three-step onboarding flow. You'll set your name, artistic discipline, and portfolio link.

**How does studio matching work?**
Fill out the Studio Needs Form with your borough preferences, budget, dates, and space requirements. StudioNow matches you with available studios that fit your criteria.

**How do I manage my subscription?**
Go to your iPhone Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → StudioNow. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel from there.

**How do I delete my account?**
Open StudioNow → Settings → Account → Delete Account. Your data will be removed within 30 days per our [Privacy Policy](/privacy/studionow/).

**I found a bug. How do I report it?**
Email us at ben@thefoghill.com with a description of the issue, your device model, and iOS version. Screenshots help!

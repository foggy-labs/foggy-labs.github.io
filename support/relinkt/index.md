## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I create a new habit streak?**
Tap the "+" button on the main screen. Give your habit a name, choose a category, and set your target frequency (daily, weekly, etc.). Your streak begins as soon as you log your first completion.

**What happens if I miss a day?**
If you miss a day, your current streak resets to zero. With Linkt Pro, you can use streak freezes to protect your streak on days you can't complete your habit.

**What are streak freezes?**
Streak freezes let you skip a day without losing your streak. Free users get a limited number; Pro users get more frequent freezes and streak insurance for additional protection.

**How do community features work?**
You can join challenges, share progress, and connect with friends who are building similar habits. Community features are available to all users, with expanded access for Pro subscribers.

**How do I manage my subscription?**
Go to your iPhone Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > Linkt. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel from there.

**How do I restore my purchases?**
Open Linkt > Settings > Restore Purchases. This will restore any active subscriptions associated with your Apple ID.

**How do I delete my account?**
Open Linkt > Settings > Account > Delete Account. Your data will be removed within 30 days per our [Privacy Policy](/privacy/relinkt/).

**I found a bug. How do I report it?**
Email us at ben@thefoghill.com with a description of the issue, your device model, and iOS version. Screenshots help!

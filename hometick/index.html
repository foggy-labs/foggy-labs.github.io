Frequently Asked Questions

Does HomeTick need an account?
No. HomeTick works entirely on your device with no sign-up, no email, and no account required. Just download and start adding tasks.
How do task reminders work?
HomeTick uses local notifications on your iPhone to alert you before tasks are due. You control the timing and frequency. No internet connection is needed for reminders to fire.
Can I track multiple homes?
Yes. You can add as many properties as you need—primary residence, vacation home, rental property—and manage tasks for each one independently.
What are seasonal checklists?
Seasonal checklists are curated sets of maintenance tasks tailored to each season. They help you stay ahead of weather-related upkeep like winterizing pipes, cleaning gutters in fall, or servicing your AC in spring.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to iPhone Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > HomeTick. You can cancel anytime and keep access until the end of your billing period.

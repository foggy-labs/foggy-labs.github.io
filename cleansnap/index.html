Frequently Asked Questions

Does CleanSnap upload my photos?
No. CleanSnap processes everything on your iPhone using Apple's Vision and Core Image frameworks. Your photos never leave your device and are never sent to any server.
Will CleanSnap modify my original photos?
No. CleanSnap creates processed copies. Your original photo is not modified unless you explicitly choose to overwrite it.
What's included in the free version?
The free tier includes basic photo cleanup tools with daily usage limits. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited cleanups, all AI enhancement tools, and priority processing.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to iPhone Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > CleanSnap. You can cancel anytime and keep access until the end of your billing period.
Can I use CleanSnap on iPad?
CleanSnap is designed for iPhone. iPad compatibility may be added in a future update.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does product cycling work?
You set a frequency for each product in your routine — for example, retinoid every 3 days or exfoliant twice a week. SkinCycle automatically builds your daily routine based on those frequencies, so you always know exactly what to apply.
Can I track multiple routines?
Yes. SkinCycle supports separate AM and PM routines out of the box. With Pro, you can create unlimited custom routines for different needs.
Does SkinCycle sync across devices?
Yes. SkinCycle uses iCloud to sync your routines, products, and streaks across all your Apple devices automatically. No account or sign-up required.
Is this medical advice?
No. SkinCycle is a personal organization tool for tracking your skincare routine. It does not provide medical or dermatological advice. Always consult a qualified professional for skin concerns.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to iPhone Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > SkinCycle. You can cancel anytime and keep access until the end of your billing period.

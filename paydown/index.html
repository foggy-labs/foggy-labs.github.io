Frequently Asked Questions

Does PayDown connect to my bank?
No. PayDown is completely offline and private. You manually enter your debts and log payments. Your financial data is stored only on your device and never uploaded anywhere.
What's the difference between Snowball and Avalanche?
Snowball pays off your lowest balance first for quick motivational wins. Avalanche pays off the highest interest rate first to save the most money. PayDown lets you compare both methods so you can choose what works for you.
How accurate are the payoff projections?
Projections are based on the balances, interest rates, and payment amounts you enter. They assume consistent monthly payments and fixed interest rates. Real-world results may vary if rates change or payments fluctuate.
What happens if I delete the app?
All your debt and payment data is stored locally on your device. Deleting the app permanently removes all data. We recommend keeping regular device backups through iCloud or your computer.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to iPhone Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > PayDown. You can cancel anytime and keep access until the end of your billing period.

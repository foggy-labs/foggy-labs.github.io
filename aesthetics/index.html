What it is

A private grooming and style consultation

Aesthetics reads your presentation the way a stylist or grooming consultant would — calmly, specifically, and with concrete direction. You leave with a personalized plan: how you already present, the archetype that fits, and the small grooming and styling moves that close the gap to your most refined version.

What it isn't

Not a contest. Not medical advice.

Aesthetics does not judge you against other people. It is a private consultation focused entirely on your own presentation. It is also not a medical, psychiatric, or identity assessment, and it will never recommend surgery, procedures, or weight changes. It is a stylistic perspective, written for adults who want to optimize their own grooming and style direction.

Frequently asked

How does the analysis work?
You take one neutral, front-facing photo. The app validates it (lighting, pose, expression, filters), then sends it via an encrypted connection to a secure AI analysis pipeline. The model returns your archetype, strengths, and a personalized optimization plan as text. The photo is immediately discarded after the analysis completes — it is not stored on any server.
What kind of app is this?
A private grooming and style consultation. It does not judge you against other people, and it is not a contest. The point is direction — toward your most refined version of yourself, written specifically for the photo in front of it.
Are my photos kept?
No. The photo you submit for analysis is discarded immediately after the report is generated. We never store it on a server, never share it, and never use it to train a model. Photos saved for your personal history live only on your iPhone, in iOS-encrypted storage.
What are the archetypes?
Ten distinct style directions — Clean Executive, Modern European, Rugged Minimalist, Model Editorial, Old Money, Soft Boy, Athletic Casual, Bohemian Creative, Nordic Cool, and Mediterranean Classic. The report identifies which archetype your presentation already fits, then gives you the specific grooming, skincare, and styling moves that sharpen that direction.
Is this medical advice?
No. Aesthetics is not a medical, psychiatric, or identity assessment. It will never recommend surgery, procedures, or weight changes. It is a stylistic perspective focused on grooming, skincare, and presentation — the things you can change with a routine and a haircut.
What if my photo is rejected?
The app validates each photo before analysis. If something is off — heavy filter, harsh lighting, side angle, expression — it tells you exactly what to fix and lets you try again. Validation runs locally before anything is sent for analysis.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Open the iOS Settings app, tap your name, then Subscriptions, then Aesthetics. You can cancel from there at any time and keep access through the end of your billing period.
Is there an age requirement?
Yes. Aesthetics is designed for adults aged 18 and over.
